About me

Hello, my name is Cameron, and I'm 22 years old. My birthday is in July. I'm currently a college student, studying to become a Video Game Designer. Some of my favorite hobbies are gaming, cooking, and working on or appreciating art, especially animation. Growing up, I didn't have the easiest childhood. But from it, I developed a love for video games and animation, which got me through tough times. My life really began when I turned 18. I moved out of my fourth foster home and into the house of a middle school friend. Now, I'm considered the sixth brother in their family. After moving in, I gave college another shot and am now working hard to balance life and my classes.
